💡 Fix the ./yt-dlp problem once and for all:
Open a terminal in this folder and run:
sudo cp yt-dlp /usr/local/bin/yt-dlp  (Linux/Mac)
After that you can just type yt-dlp anywhere — no more ./ needed!

On Windows? Add the folder to your PATH, or just use the command with .\yt-dlp in the box below.
